Learn the art of portrait photography with expert John Smith. This course covers lighting, composition, and post-processing techniques to create stunning portraits.

Course Blueprint

1. Introduction to Portrait Photography

  • Understanding portrait photography
  • Choosing the right equipment
  • Setting up your camera

2. Lighting Techniques

3. Composition and Posing

  • Directing your subject
  • Posing for different moods

4. Post-Processing

  • Editing in Adobe Lightroom
  • Retouching portraits
  • Exporting for print and web
